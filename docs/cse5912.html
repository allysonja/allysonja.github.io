Homework 2

For the first image, this is a photo of me at the National Space Museum with the Curiosity rover. I spliced this image with GIMP on top of an image of the surface of Mars. The second photo is of me as a jellyfish with an exhibit of jellyfish from the aquarium. I used Pixlr to transpose the images and affect the transparency. The third image is a photo I took for the Getty Museum Challenge, where you attempt to look like a piece of art that is in the museum. I chose the portrait Mrs. Charles E. Inches (Louise Pomeroy) by John Singer Sargent, as seen in the forth photo, and used Pixlr to edit the photo to make it more grainy and painting-like.
